# One Piece

About the Series
*One Piece* is a Japanese anime and manga created by *Eiichiro Oda*.  
It follows the adventures of *Monkey D. Luffy*, a young pirate with the dream of becoming the *Pirate King* by finding the legendary treasure known as *One Piece*.

---

Why I Like This Series
- The story is rich, emotional, and exciting.  
- The world-building is massive and full of unique islands and cultures.  
- Each Straw Hat Pirate has a strong personality and inspiring backstory.  
- It teaches lessons about *dreams, friendship, and loyalty*.

---

Favorite Characters
1. *Monkey D. Luffy* – The energetic captain.  
2. *Roronoa Zoro* – The loyal swordsman.  
3. *Sanji* – The skilled cook.  
4. *Nami* – The smart navigator.  
5. *Tony Tony Chopper* – The adorable doctor.
